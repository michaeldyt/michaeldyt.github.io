    </div>
    <div class="home-card-grid">
      <article class="home-card">
        <p class="home-meta">ASC25 · 2025.3 - 2025.5</p>
        <h3>Optimization on AlphaFold3 Model</h3>
        <p>Participated in optimizing AlphaFold3 by designing an MPI-based distributed inference framework. Leveraging profiling results, implemented high-frequency low-level operators with LIBXSMM and achieved up to 50x inference speedup.</p>
      </article>
      <article class="home-card">
        <p class="home-meta">ASC26 · 2025.12 - 2026.5</p>
        <h3>Optimization on World Model Inference</h3>
        <ul>
          <li>Accelerated inference for UnifoLM-WMA-0 and LeCun's LeWorldModel under given precision constraints.</li>
          <li>Applied low-precision quantization, full-graph compilation, dynamic pipeline simplification, multi-GPU parallelization, communication optimization, and synchronization optimization.</li>
          <li>Achieved around 8x and 100x inference speedup on the two world models respectively.</li>
          <li>Designed a safe and flexible orchestra agentic coding pipeline based on OpenCode and Apptainer for stable auto-optimization in container environments.</li>
        </ul>
      </article>
